RPG Project

Made with Godot Game Engine written in GDScript. Art assets and animations made in Aseprite.

~ Inspired by Stardew Valley, Harvest Moon and Animal Crossing, I have been steadily working on a mobile restaurant management role playing game.

~ With character customization players can choose their appearance and then customize their restaurant menu. By travelling to different locations such as the grocery store, players can buy ingredients and create new foods.

~ Future implementations will include a dialogue system and relationship system where players can form romantic and platonic relationships.
